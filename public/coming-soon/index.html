Frequently asked

Quick answers.

Can I sign up now if you haven't launched yet?

Yes. You can register today, complete your profile and configure your availability — all before we go live publicly to patients. When we launch, your profile is ready to take bookings on day one.

When is MyDoctors360 launching to patients?

Soon. Founding doctors get advance notice and go live the moment we open the doors to patients.

What does it cost?

Registering is free. You won't be charged anything until the platform goes live, and founding doctors receive exclusive pricing that's locked in for life — full details shared as we get closer.

Who can register?

Verified, regulated doctors and clinicians practising privately across the UK, Ireland, Italy, Spain, Germany, France, Portugal, Poland and Turkey. Clinics and group practices welcome.

How is this different from existing platforms?

We're building from the ground up with input from working doctors. Modern tooling, transparent pricing, multilingual reach and genuinely useful automation — not a directory bolted onto a billing system.
